 Note: In order to use in-camera HDR feature, you need to update your camera's firmware. You would need PC app to do so. When you try this, Adobe AIR Updater might ask you to update your AIR to 20.0. However, 20.0.0.204 runtime might not be compatible with your Mac app (it did not work on mine — OS 10.10.5). If your RICOH THETA app stop working after Adobe AIR update, I suggest uninstall ‘Adobe AIR 20 runtime’ by using ‘Adobe AIR Uninstaller’ (this app is in your Utility folder), then re-install 19. You can download ‘Adobe AIR 19 runtime’ from Adobe archive, helpx.adobe.com/air/kb/archived-air-sdk-version.html // When this happens you probably also need to re-install your RICOH THETA app… And Adobe AIR will ask you again to update your AIR 19 to 20, now you know what not to do.. so, please be careful.
